Macnification
=============

`Macnification <http://www.orbicule.com/macnification/>`_ is a Mac OS X
application for organizing, editing, analyzing and annotating
microscopic images, designed for ease of use. It is being developed by
`Orbicule <http://www.orbicule.com>`_.

Macnification uses Bio-Formats to read files in some formats, including
Gatan DM3, ICS, ImagePro SEQ, ImagePro IPW, Metamorph STK, OME-TIFF and
Zeiss LSM.
