OME-TIFF
========

Extensions: .ome.tiff

Developer: LOCI

Support
-------

SCIFIO: |yes|

Export: |yes|

Officially Supported Versions: 2003FC, 2007-06, 2008-02, 2008-09, 2009-09, 2010-04, 2010-06

Supported Metadata Fields: `OME-TIFF Reader <http://dev.loci.wisc.edu/trac/software/wiki/OMETiffReader>`_

We currently have: 

-  An `OME-TIFF specification
   document <http://www.loci.wisc.edu/ome/ome-tiff-spec.html>`_ (from
   2006 Oct 19, in HTML)
-  Numerous OME-TIFF datasets
-  The ability to produce additional datasets
