Andor Bio-Imaging Division (ABD) TIFF
=====================================

Extensions: .tif

Developer: Andor Bioimaging Department

Owner: `Andor Technology <http://www.andor.com/>`_

Support
-------

SCIFIO: |no|

Export: |no|

Supported Metadata Fields: `FluoviewReader <http://dev.loci.wisc.edu/trac/software/wiki/FluoviewReader>`_

We currently have: 

-  an ABD-TIFF specification document (from 2005 Nov, in PDF)
-  three ABD-TIFF datasets

Additional Information
----------------------

Source Code: :source:`FluoviewReader <components/bio-formats/src/loci/formats/in/FluoviewReader.java>`

Notes: 

With a few minor exceptions, the ABD-TIFF format is identical to the
Fluoview TIFF format.

**Please note that while we have specification documents for this
format, we are not able to distribute them to third parties.**
