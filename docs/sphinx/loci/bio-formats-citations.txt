Citations
=========

To cite Bio-Formats in your publication, you can use the following
`reference <http://jcb.rupress.org/content/189/5/777>`_:

    Linkert et al. 2010. Metadata matters: access to image data in the
    real world. J Cell Biol. 189:777-782. PMID: 20513764

See `this page <http://www.ome-xml.org/wiki/CitingOME>`_ for more
information on citing OME tools.
